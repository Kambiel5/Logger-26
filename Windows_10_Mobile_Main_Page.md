<h1>Logger 26: High-Performance POTA Logging</h1>

<blockquote>
  <strong>The lightweight, high-performance logging terminal designed for the modern portable operator.</strong>
</blockquote>

<p>Built to target the <strong>UWP standards</strong>, Logger 26 offers a "zero-bloat" experience for <strong>POTA</strong>, <strong>SOTA</strong>, or mobile <strong>HF/VHF</strong> operations.</p>

<hr />

<h3>Core Features</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Rapid QSO Entry</strong><br />
  Log callsigns, frequency, mode, RST, and power with minimal taps. Timestamps are fully automated so you can stay focused on the pileup.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Smart Band & Mode Detection</strong><br />
  Intelligently maps frequencies to bands to reduce manual entry errors and speed up your workflow.</li>
  
  <li><strong>CW Haptic Engine</strong><br />
  A unique tactile feedback system. Confirm callsigns and status signals via <strong>Morse code vibration</strong>—perfect for bright sunlight where screens are hard to read.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Session History</strong><br />
  View and verify your 5 most recent QSOs at a glance for quick on-the-fly corrections.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Windows 10 Live Tiles</strong><br />
  Integration with the Metro UI allows you to view your latest contacts directly on your home screen.</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<h3>Why Logger 26 is Different</h3>

<p>While other apps are filled with trackers and insecure storage, <strong>Logger 26</strong> is built from the ground up for the security-conscious operator:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Hardware-Backed Encryption</strong><br />
  Your QRZ credentials aren't just saved; they are encrypted using <strong>AES-GCM</strong> within the Windows Password Vault.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Zero Telemetry</strong><br />
  No ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. Your data stays strictly between you and QRZ.com.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Native & Modern</strong><br />
  Optimized for the <strong>Windows 10 Mobile Metro UI</strong>. It feels like a native part of your OS, not an afterthought.</li>
  
  <li><strong>Privacy-First</strong><br />
  Forced <strong>HTTPS (TLS 1.3)</strong> for all transmissions.</li>
</ul>

<h3> Distribution & Installation</h3>

<p>Logger 26 removes the "digital friction" of logging, allowing you to focus on the radio, not the screen.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Format:</strong> Distributed as a lightweight <code>.appxbundle</code> <strong>universal* binary</strong> for manual sideloading.</li>
  <li><strong>Compatibility:</strong> Designed Mobile-first for ARM, with an experimental X86 compatibility layer.</li>
